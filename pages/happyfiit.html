Happy Fiit

The concept was to create a simplified fitness app that offers a range of exercises for each muscle group in the body, and promotes overall fitness. We aimed to create a platform that tracks your physical and mental well-being, enabling you to be in control of your health.

We wanted to build a user-friendly application adept at offering tailored exercises based on user input, integrating features that simplify the fitness journey. We believe fitness doesn't have to involve lengthy workouts; rather, it's the consistent day-to-day routines that change us for the better.

As a user, I want the app to suggest exercises based on the goals I choose , enabling me to perform timed exercises and track my progress, so that I can effectively target specific areas and see tangible improvements in my fitness journey.
