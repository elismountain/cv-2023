About Me

Hello, I’m Olga, I am an artist, jewelry maker, and frontend developer based in Brighton United Kingdom.

I decided to learn web development a year ago when I needed to build a website for one of my artistic projects. In a very short time, I realized that I like it a lot, as it gives me the possibility to challenge myself and use a mix of technologies and creativity.

I started my journey a year ago when I signed up for the Front End Developer course at RS School. RS School is a free-of-charge and community-based education program conducted by The Rolling Scopes developer community. The mentors and trainers of the school are front-end and javascript developers from different companies and countries. It was a very challenging year, sometimes I thought, I couldn’t move any further, and it took me days and numerous attempts to solve some problem, but in the end, it resulted in numerous projects I am happy with. Please, see my projects on my GitHub.

Here are the technologies I use:

  • HTML, CSS (Less)
  • JavaScript (ES6+)
  • Figma
  • GitHub
  • Node.js
